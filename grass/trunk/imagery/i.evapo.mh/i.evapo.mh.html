DESCRIPTION

i.evapo.mh calculates the reference evapotranspiration (ET) after Hargreaves (1985) and the Modified Hargreaves (2001).

SEE ALSO

i.evapo.pt, i.evapo.pm, i.evapo.time, r.sun
