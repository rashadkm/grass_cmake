DESCRIPTION

i.eb.soilheatflux calculates the soil heat flux approximation (g0) after [1]. Main reference for implementation is [3]. It takes input of Albedo, NDVI, Surface Skin temperature, Net Radiation (see r.sun), time of satellite overpass, and a flag for the Roerink empirical modification from the HAPEX-Sahel experiment.
